# CYBERWISE

CYBERWISE focuses on cybersecurity awareness and learning. Its purpose is to help people recognize risks, practise safer behaviour, and understand their role before an incident occurs.

## What This Means For NIS2COMPASS

NIS2COMPASS uses the CYBERWISE role to publish beginner-friendly learning material and privacy-aware guidance for aggregate awareness metrics. Public material avoids individual risk scores, recipient lists, campaign configurations, personal performance records, and claims that a single training score proves secure behaviour.

> **Public software position**
>
> No individual-scoring or gamified-training product is named by NIS2COMPASS on this page. The public CYberSynchrony deliverables index identifies awareness and training outputs at project level; this website does not infer public availability of a private platform or product from a deliverable title.
