# Awareness And Phishing Metrics Guide

Awareness work helps people recognize and report risky behavior. Metrics should be aggregate and privacy-safe.

## Minimum Outputs

- Role map.
- Training material.
- Attendance record.
- Knowledge check, where practical.
- Baseline metric.
- Follow-up metric.
- Aggregate summary.

## Public-Safe Reporting

Use aggregate counts and percentages. Do not publish individual names, emails, click records, or disciplinary information.
